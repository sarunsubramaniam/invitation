Vritham (Kickstarting Transformation)

Groom needs to seek blessings from ancestors,his parents & family to end his BrahmaCharya (celibacy), to move to the next stage - Grihastha (family life). A sacred thread with turmeric is tied on the groom's hand to wardoff evil spirits, Same happened with bride in her home.

Time: (03-02-2021, at 8.00am)

Nichayadhartham

Nichayathartham is the formal engagement ceremony that takes place in the presence of all elders of both the families, Both the bride/groom’s families announce their final agreement to the wedding.

Time: (03-02-2021, at 6.30pm)

Kaashi Yatra

This is a tradition where bride’s father will entice the groom to marry his loving daughter instead of leaving to Kaashi to attain spiritual enlightenment.

Singing Laali and Swinging Oonjal

Bride and groom will be seated together on a swing to ease them and make comfortable. The Sumangalis will offer banana & milk and wave coloured rice balls around them and throw in all directions while singing the laaaaaallliiiii songs. The Oonjal’s to and fro motion symbolises the ups and downs of life through which they must stick together.

Kanya Dhaanam

The bride sits on the lap of her father, who holds a ‘thamboola’ (betel leaves and areca nut) in his palms. She then places her palms holding a coconut on her father’s palms. As the groom receives the bride’s hand from her father, the bride’s mother pours water over her daughter’s hand, which is made to fall on the ground like a ‘dhaara’. This ceremony is called ‘dhaarai vaarthu kodukkal’ in Tamil.

Saptapati(Walk around the Agni Saatchi)

The couple walks around the sacred fire in three rounds. On the first round, groom places bride’s right foot on the Ammi to place the Metti. On the second round, groom repeats this on the left foot. They complete the final round to symbolise their union as a husband and wife complete and full.

Jaanavasam(Mappillai Azhaippu)

The groom is brought in a procession from the temple on a horse reigned chariot, jam-packed with notorious childrens , surrounded by family members and friends. The nadhaswaram band leads the way, with bride’s parents welcoming the mappillai to the marriage.

Time: (03-02-2021, at 6.00pm)

Reception

Sit back and relish the heartwarming music concert by legendary Veena artist Shri.Rajesh Vaidhya ! Click snaps with the couple and shower them your blessings with the presence rather with presents.

Time: (03-02-2021, at 7.00pm)

Maalai matral

This is an extremely cheerful ceremony where the bride and groom are hoisted on the shoulders of their respected uncles and compete to exchange garlands thrice which symbolises their unification

Pravaram

The ‘Gothras’ of both the bride and the groom are announced loudly by the priest along with their lineage up to three generations - father - grandfather - great grandfather

Mangalya Dhaaranam

This is the most significant moment of the wedding.Dressed in traditional Madisaar saree, The Bride makes her way accompanied by her sister in law. To the beat of Thavils and music of the Nadhaswaram, the groom ties the “Thiru Mangalyam '' around bride’s neck , signifying the long lasting love.

Nalangu

Bride and groom take part in a series of fun games. Nalangu features a variety of games based on the values of understanding, compromising and complementing.
